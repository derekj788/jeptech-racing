About

Built on
old iron

JepTech Racing is a Mooresville, NC-based workshop specializing exclusively in vintage Japanese motorcycles. We work on the bikes that matter — CB750s, GS-series Suzukis, early Kawasakis, and the rest of the golden era that defined what motorcycles could be.

No fluff, no shortcuts. Just careful, technically precise work done by someone who actually rides and wrenches on these machines. Whether it's a carburetor that's never run right or a full engine teardown, it gets done correctly.

Services

What we
do

Focused, methodical work on vintage Japanese machines. We don't work on everything — we work on this.

01

Carburetor
Rebuild & Tune

Full disassembly, ultrasonic cleaning, and rebuild of CV and slide-type carburetors. Synchronization, jetting, pilot circuit work.

  • Keihin CV carbs (VB, PD series)
  • Mikuni VM & BS series
  • Full bench sync & road tune
  • Pilot / main circuit overhaul
02

General
Maintenance

Routine service and tune-ups to keep vintage machines running as they should. Valve adjustments, ignition timing, fluid service.

  • Valve clearance adjustment
  • Ignition points & timing
  • Oil & filter service
  • Brake inspection & service
03

Engine
Rebuilds

Top-end and full engine teardowns. Cam chain, valve seals, head gaskets, bore and piston work on vintage inline-fours and twins.

  • Top-end refreshes
  • Full engine disassembly
  • Cam chain replacement
  • Bore & piston assessment
